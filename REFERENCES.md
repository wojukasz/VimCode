### LazyVim Concepts

VimCode follows LazyVim's keybinding organization:

- **`<leader>` key** - Space as leader for discoverability
- **Prefix patterns** - Organized by category (`f*`, `s*`, `c*`, etc.)
- **Mnemonic bindings** - Easy to remember (`f` = file, `s` = search, etc.)
- **Consistent navigation** - `[]` for previous/next navigation
